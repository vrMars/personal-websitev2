Nothing-But-Net

Nothing-But-Net is an arcade style mobile game with one simple goal: put the basketball into the net by any means possible. The gameplay is based around the idea of free-hand drawing bridges, pathways, and walls to carefully and quickly guide a basketball into a net. The game uses a custom built physics-engine and touch interface.
