Nothing_but_net is an arcade style basketball mobile-game. The objective of the game is to draw physics enabled paths such that the basketball is able to bounce into the net, whilst avoiding obstacles. Written by a team of two programmers, this game uses a custom physics engine to interpret user drawn objects and collisions.
